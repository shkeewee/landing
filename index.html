About the Event

Join us at the Breathe and Dream Conference on October 17-19, 2024, at Golden Tulip, Cluj-Napoca, Romania. This event focuses on obstructive lung diseases (Day Theme) and sleep medicine (Night Theme). Connect with experts and peers to explore the latest advancements in these fields.

Obstructive Lung Diseases (Day Theme)

Day 1 focuses on innovations in obstructive lung diseases, covering the latest advancements in asthma and COPD diagnosis and treatment, as well as workshops on personalized medicine and new biologic therapies.

Sleep Medicine (Night Theme)

Day 2 explores breakthroughs in sleep medicine, including novel approaches for managing sleep disorders like obstructive sleep apnea, insomnia, and more. Hands-on sessions will highlight the latest technologies in sleep studies.

The Future of Medicine - Digital Health and Beyond

Day 3 discusses the impact of digital health on managing obstructive lung diseases and sleep medicine, with topics on telehealth, wearable technologies, and AI applications. A closing panel will focus on fostering international collaboration in medical research.
